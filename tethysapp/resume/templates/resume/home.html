    Early days

    The early days in Bolivia were fast and full of energy. I was raised in several elementary schools while my parents settle up. Early days were hard, but great, and I think they shape the way I am right now. It is hard sometimes to avoid fate and casuality when the different customs and experiences of childhood are embedded in the heart of the adult...
    I will say that experiences shape one's will and aspect throughout life, and childhood is not the exception. The outcome of it depends of one. These days will be a beautiful memory in my heart when I remember them on a chair looking to all the years that I have live. No more than a mere point of existance in the earth

    Puerto Maldonado

    Puerto will always be one of the most precious memories. It was on 2017 when I decided to go to this town that it is beautiful and has some melancholy that is hidden in the people of the palce . Faces and dust in the miner men, and sadness and frustration in the faces of the lovely women. I never has been amazed byt the complexity of the culture of a town, but I guess maldonado gained my admiration.
    Miners working the whole day and during the night alcohol and cigarettes for everyone was the daily schedule. Working on the mine was hard, but also rewarding when gold was found. If such treasure would last the next day ...
